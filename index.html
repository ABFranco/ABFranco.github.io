FreeWheel

FreeWheel is an Android application developed in a team of 5 as a class final project. The app had a target audience of users with mobile disabilities, such as wheelchair users, and assisted them to find public transportation methods. I was the primary Front-End programmer as well as implement Google's Maps, Places, and Directions APIs. During the project, we had to conduct user interviews, rapidly prototype, code, and eventually present our project in a public showcase.
